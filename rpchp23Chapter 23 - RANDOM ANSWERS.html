Chapter 23 - RANDOM ANSWERS

Strength training is the process of asking your body to fight back against something more than gravity. This results in more muscle, more strength, higher bone density and more ability to resist force in the future.

Running is a form of exercise, transport, self defence, hobby and sport.

The best strength training is training that applies the right amount of force, in the right places, at the right time to produce the highest result. Anything more is überflüssig/for fun/practicing sadism.

Sleep is the most powerful performance enhancing drug.

In the first few weeks and months of regular exercising, the results you gain are likely to be the most significant % increase you’ll ever experience. This applies to learning new skills too.

Using a metronome to slow your repetitions when your training to build muscle, strength or both is helpful and humbling.

A cold muscle is like a dry sponge.

Training hard whilst leaving 1-2 repetitions in the tank will mean you can train more often and ultimately harder.

Symptoms of pain are often not the cause of pain. And the amount of pain does not always equal the amount of damage.

Think of that next time you stub your toe and feel like the world is ending.

Movement can come natural when you embrace the the environment you live in. Ocean - surfing, snow - skiing, mountains - hiking, trails - running. If you’re not quite there, there will surely be a gym around the corner.
