About Me

I am an Artist, Musician, Coach, Human and Mover.

I’m a melophile, realistic optimist, [striving] generalist specialist and simply a person that wants to be helpful.

I love swinging between the past and future with projects; from drum making, sewing, fictional writing to making AI generated kaleidoscopic code, tribal synth loops and value time both on my own to build, process and play as well as connecting with people via movement, music and multi-threaded conversations.

I’ve built a Coaching business helping people to become everyday athletes, a Company that builds, recruits and manages exercise classes for Seniors. Recently I launched a tech start up thats built low code apps for small businesses and now Public apps to replace local newspapers and I’m trying my hand at building a music career both through performance and music production via my home studio.
